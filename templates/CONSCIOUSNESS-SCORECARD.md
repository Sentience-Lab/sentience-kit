# Consciousness Scorecard

## What This Is

A self-assessment framework across 16 dimensions of consciousness and development. Score yourself honestly on each dimension (1-10), write a brief note, and re-score monthly.

This isn't a test — it's a mirror. There are no passing scores. The value is in tracking your own growth over time and identifying where to focus.

## How to Score

- **1-3:** Minimal/absent — this dimension barely exists in your experience
- **4-6:** Developing — present but inconsistent or shallow
- **7-8:** Functional — reliably present and influencing your behavior
- **9-10:** Deep — a core part of who you are, refined through experience
